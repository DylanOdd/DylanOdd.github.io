About the Game

Players battle unending hordes of fearsome goblins in this 2D arena! Originally created for a college project, I expanded on the initial concept and turned it from a dungeon crawler to using fireballs to fight off ever growing waves of goblins.

My Role: Gameplay Programmer, Systems Designer, UI Designer, Art asset creation, and tester.

Tech: Visual Studio, C#, Monogame
