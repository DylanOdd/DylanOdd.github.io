About the Game

Learn the powers of the ancient druids and make your way through the abandoned temple. Beware the traps they left behind and use your new powers to escape! This game was made as our end of year college project, where I took on the role of lead level designer. I also helped with some of the programming for the interactive objects, along with character design.

My Role: Gameplay Programmer, Level Designer, Art asset creation, and Tester.

Tech: Godot
