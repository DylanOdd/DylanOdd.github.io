About the Game

Navigate the maze to escape the Killer Robots! Avoid their searchlights and complete the state of the art Robot Detection System to survive! Originally created for a high school project, I expanded on the initial concepts and added powerups, created background music, and several other technical improvements.

My Role: Gameplay Programmer, Systems Designer, UI Designer, Art asset creation, and tester.

Tech: Pygame, Python, MySQL
