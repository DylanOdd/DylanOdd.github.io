About the Game

The player must destroy their opponents by ramming their balloon powered house into them. (Inspired by Williams Electronics' Joust). This was one of my college projects. I was responsible for the programming, UI, and art acquisition.

My Role: Gameplay Programmer, Level Designer, Art asset creation, and Tester.

Tech: Visual Studio, C#, Monogame
