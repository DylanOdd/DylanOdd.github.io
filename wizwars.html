About the Game

Players take the roll of Alchemist Wizards in this multiplayer battle game, laying traps in a maze environment. (Inspired by Hudsonsoft's Bomberman). Originally created for a college project, I expanded on the initial concepts and added powerups, created background music, and several other technical improvements.

My Role: Gameplay Programmer, Systems Designer, UI Designer, Art asset creation, and tester.

Tech: Visual Studio, C#, Monogame
